Pancake Recipe

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter. It is a type of batter bread. Click on the image, to find the recipe.

Number of servings

Total Ingredient Quantity for 1 Serving(s): 1 cup

Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 2 tablespoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1 big
Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and vegetable oil.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (do not over-mix).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake and the bottom is golden-brown, then flip and cook the other side until golden-brown.
  6. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  7. Enjoy your pancakes!
